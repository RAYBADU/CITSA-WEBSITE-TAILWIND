WHO WE ARE

About CITSA-UCC

Empowering Tech Leaders at UCC

The Computing and Information Technology Students Association (CITSA) is the premier student body representing all Computer Science and Information Technology students at the University of Cape Coast.

Our Vision

To be the leading tech community that shapes the next generation of technology leaders in Africa.

Our Mission

To foster innovation and professional growth through practical learning, industry connections, and collaborative projects.

Our Community

A diverse network of tech enthusiasts, innovators, and future industry leaders working together to achieve excellence.
