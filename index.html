WHO WE ARE

About CITSA-UCC

Empowering Tech Leaders at UCC

The Computing and Information Technology Students Association (CITSA) is the premier student body representing all Computer Science and Information Technology students at the University of Cape Coast.

Our Vision

To be the leading tech community that shapes the next generation of technology leaders in Africa.

Our Mission

To foster innovation and professional growth through practical learning, industry connections, and collaborative projects.

Our Community

A diverse network of tech enthusiasts, innovators, and future industry leaders working together to achieve excellence.

MESSAGE FROM HOD

Department Head's Address

Dr. Abdul-Lateef Yussif

Head of Department

"For more than 25 years, computing courses have been provided for students from various departments by the computer centre. With the recent developments and trends in the information technology (IT), the University of Cape Coast established an academic department known as the Department of Computer Science and Information Technology." "The primary focus of this department is teaching and research in computing. Furthermore the need for trained computer scientists and information technologists is one of the challenges that face the rapidly growing Ghanaian economy..."
